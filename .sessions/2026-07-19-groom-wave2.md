## 💡 Session idea

**A groom-ladder succession-pointer advisory lint.** This session hit the failure
manually: when a newer `docs/planning/*-idea-groom*.md` supersedes an older one, the
older doc keeps `> **Status:** \`plan\`` and looks live/current unless a human
remembers to add a `Succeeded by` pointer — and the new doc silently orphans until
linked. Idea: an advisory-only `check` finding that, given two or more
`*-idea-groom*.md` docs both carrying `Status: plan`, flags the older one(s) that
lack a `Succeeded by` reference to a newer groom. Enforce-don't-exhort form of the
manual reachability+succession link added here; distinct from `check_docs`'s reachable
check (which only tests link-reachability, not supersession semantics), from S3 (which
counts un-groomed `💡` lines), and from S4 (baton-anchor resolution). Deduped against
`docs/ideas/` (no groom-succession or baton-freshness idea exists) and the S-list.
Small/decided-lane advisory; a good grooming pick once a third groom doc lands.
