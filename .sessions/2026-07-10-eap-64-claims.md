## Decision (decide-and-flag)

ONE kit-owned convention with two surfaces: ORDER claims stay `claimed-by:` on your own
heartbeat orders line (one-writer-per-file, already enforced); WORK/lane claims are
one-file-per-claim under **`control/claims/`** (the measured 0%-conflict layout; under
`control/` so claims ride the CI fast lane and land on main fast). Compat window:
legacy homes (`docs/owner/claims/`, root `claims/`) auto-detected + scanned in place with
a `claims-legacy-location` nudge — every claims finding is advisory-by-contract, so no
adopter's existing claims can red a gate on upgrade; `claims_dir` config key (default
`control/claims`) pins a deliberate different home (pinned = canonical, no nudge).
Flagged constants: 72h work-claim horizon (vs 24h order horizon — session-close deletion
makes age ≈ orphan, but weekend gaps are real); legacy list seeded as exactly the two
observed homes.
