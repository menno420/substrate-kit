## 💡 Session idea

`--verify-only`, two repo checkers, `check --strict`) whose results a
session then hand-copies into its card — every cut re-assembles the
same shell history. A `scripts/release_preflight.py` that runs the
whole suite and exits non-zero on any failure would turn the runbook's
checklist into one command with one verdict (enforce, don't exhort),
and its printed summary block would be paste-ready for the session
card. Dedup: distinct from `changelog-unreleased-structure-checker`
(one artifact's shape) and `upgrade-checklist-release-json-placement`
(adopter-side); nothing bundles the cut-time suite today.
