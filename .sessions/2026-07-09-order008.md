### 💡 Session idea

The OWNER-ACTION `VERIFIED-NEEDED` field and `docs/CAPABILITIES.md` are two
halves of one loop that nothing currently closes: when an ask's
VERIFIED-NEEDED cites a wall that is NOT yet in CAPABILITIES.md, the wall
should be appended there in the same session (and vice versa — a
CAPABILITIES.md wall entry is a ready-made VERIFIED-NEEDED citation).
A future `check` advisory could cross-reference the two files and nudge
when an ask cites an unrecorded wall — turning every owner-ask into a
capability-ledger contribution for free.
