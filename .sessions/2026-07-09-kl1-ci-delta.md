## 💡 Session idea

Give `check` an explicit card selector — `check --session-log <file>` plus, in the planted
`substrate-gate.yml`, PR-diff-aware selection (gate on the card the PR *adds/changes*, not
newest-by-mtime). The mtime heuristic silently degrades on fresh checkouts (this session had
to ship a `touch`-from-git shim in CI); every consumer inherits that trap via
`live_ci_workflow`, and an engine-level selector deletes the whole shim class kit-wide.
